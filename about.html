About Percentile In CEE

For example ,

there are total number of 30,000 students who appeared CEE exam.
Now you scored a rank of 9,000, then your CEE percentile will be:
YOUR CEE PERCENTILE = [ 1 - ( 9,000 / 30,000) ] * 100 = 70 percentile


This percentile means that 70 percent students who gave CEE exam shall have score below you.

According to MEC recent notice , A aspirant who got more than 50 percentile are eligible for UG health profession .
Now, there is no any passing marks . You must be in 50 to 99.99 percentile .

But remember one thing , There is no any passing marks but your marks determine your rank and your rank determine your percentile .

< b style="font-size:30px"> Higher percentile = higer chance to get good collage


Percentile calculation formula,
[1-( your rank / total exam appearing students ) ] * 100
